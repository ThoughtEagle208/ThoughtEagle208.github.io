Kartinator

This karting game is very simplistic with a polytopian artwork style. It consists of a reletively short oval track and currently only supports single player. I am working on an update to add more elaborate tracks with multiple laps, at the moment this is just a time trial. Im hoping to add AI and multiplayer support in the future.

If you have any suggestions or ideas for future game updates let me know in an email or send a message in the discord. Send emails to thoughteagle208gaming@gmail.com. Don't forget to put the name of the game as your subject.
